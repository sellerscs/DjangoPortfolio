What Is Esports For Edu?

Mission

Our mission is to create high-quality, student-centered experiences through scholastic esports so ALL students can use esports as a platform to grow socially, emotionally, and academically.

Vision

To ensure that ALL students possess the knowledge and skills needed to be society's game changers: educated, productive, and empathetic individuals.

Values

Learning. Community. Opportunity. Diversity.
